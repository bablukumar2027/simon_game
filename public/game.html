How to Play Simon Says

  • Press the START button to begin the game.
  • Watch and memorize the sequence of lights that Simon shows.
  • Repeat the sequence by clicking the colored buttons in the same order.
  • Each level adds one more step to the sequence.
  • If you make a mistake, the game ends and your score is recorded.
  • Try to beat your high score by reaching the highest level possible!
  • You can reset the game anytime with the RESET button.
  • Use keyboard keys 1-4 as shortcuts for the colored buttons.
